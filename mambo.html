About Mambo

Mambo is a 7 year old rescued miniature pinscher our family adopted when he was a little puppy. Although he's a small dog, he has the character and attitude of a big dog (we think he believies he's a doberman...!) and is super special with "his" things, he will move his bed wherever he fancies in the house always taking the best spots! He loves the sand and green grass, will always be near you if there is food and he's always ready for a nap.
